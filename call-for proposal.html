Submission Instructions

The proposal should include (1) 1-page cover sheet with budget; (2) 2-page research plan; (3) 1-page references for the research plan; (4) NSF/NIH style biosketch for each investigator. Further details are below.

  • Cover sheet 
  • Research plan 
  • References 
  • Biosketch

The 1-page cover sheet should include the following items:

    o Investigator: The names, institutions, and the departments of the investigators.
    o Title: The proposal title.
    o Research Goal: A one-paragraph, non-technical executive summary of the research goals.
    o Budget: Budget requests, and the total funding being requested.

The 2-page research plan should include the following items:

    o Scientific Question(s): What scientific question does this proposal address? Why is it important? What will be learned through the proposed research?
    o Research Strategy: A description of the rationale for the research methods that will be used to achieve the research objectives.
    o Expected Outcomes: A description of the expected outcomes and impact of the research, including potential contributions to the field. 

Throughout your application, you will reference all relevant publications for the concepts underlying your research and your methods.

NSF/NIH style biosketch for each faculty investigator.
