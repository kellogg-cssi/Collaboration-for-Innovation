Fall 2023 Collaboration for Innovation Research Award Call for Proposal [Download]

The Center for Science of Science & Innovation at Kellogg (CSSI)
Laboratory for Innovation Science at Harvard (LISH)

$200,000 in funds are available for innovative, high-risk, and enabling collaborative research seed funding in Fall 2023. The awards program offers unrestricted funds to support research at academic institutions and non-profit organizations in any areas with our mission to promote collaboration and innovation. It will support 20 projects for $5000 - $10000. Key aspects of the proposal funding mechanism are as follows:

Submission Instructions

The proposal should include the following items only:

  • Cover Sheet 1-page cover sheet with budget (see below);
  • Research Plan 2-page research plan (see below)
  • Reference 1-page for references cited in the research plan (Optional)
  • Biosketch NSF/NIH style biosketch for each faculty investigator.
  • Cover sheet 
  • Research plan 
  • References (opt)

The 1-page cover sheet should include the following items:
    o The names, academic rank, and the departments of the investigators;
    o The proposal title;
    o A one-paragraph, non-technical executive summary of the research goals (the summaries from all proposals submitted will be shared with the donor);
    o Budget: List trainees that will be supported by this award and for how long, their salaries, other budget requests, and the total funding being requested.  

The 2-page research plan should include the following items:
    o Scientific Questions: What scientific question does this proposal address? Why is it important? What will be learned through the proposed research? Why is the project too risky and/or not well suited for established funding mechanisms?
    o Research Plan: Provide a compelling and well justified research plan that can be completed within 18 months.
    o Research Team: Summarize the expertise that each team member brings to the project. Also, briefly discuss whether/how this represents a new collaboration, an expanded collaboration, and/or a new research direction for an existing collaboration.
    o Measures of Success: Discuss the ways in which this proposal will (1) affect future research directions for the PIs, (2) increase the likelihood of obtaining external funding (identify at least one funding agency), and/or (3) increase scholars’ visibility in their domains.
    o Figures. 

1-page may be used to list references cited in the research plan (optional)

Review and Post-Award Requirements

  • We expect to make award decisions by Dec 1st, 2023.
  • Deliverables are expected to include a one-paragraph, non-technical executive summary of the research accomplishments.
